What matters most

The Real Wins in Life

There are things in this world that look like success — money, things, titles, status. And then there are the things that actually fill you up when the room gets quiet and the world stops spinning for a moment. The real wins. The ones you cannot put a price on.

You are one of mine, Mom. You have always been one of mine.

A mother's love is one of the only things in this life that does not come with a receipt, a return policy, or an expiration date. It just is. It has always just been. And I have not always said it enough — but I feel it every single day.

"Time don't give itself freely — so every moment we have together is a gift I do not take lightly."
— Lil Renie

I have been thinking a lot lately about what really matters. Not the noise. Not the rushing. Not the to-do lists that never end. But the quiet moments. The phone calls. The times we just sit together and breathe the same air and know that we are safe because we are with each other.

Those moments — that is where the real treasure is. And I want more of them with you.

"Some things only a mother and daughter understand."

What money can't buy

Memories That Last Forever

You cannot buy the feeling of being known — truly known — by someone who has watched you grow from the very beginning. You cannot buy the comfort of a voice that has always been home to you. You cannot buy the kind of love that shows up even when it is hard, even when life gets in the way, even when the miles or the years or the weight of everything tries to stretch the distance between two hearts.

That is what we have. That is what I never want to lose.

I want us to make more memories, Mom. Not big ones — not expensive ones. Just real ones. The kind that live in your chest long after the day is over. A meal together. A laugh that goes too long. A conversation that starts about one thing and ends up somewhere neither of us expected. Those are the ones I treasure most.

Because time does not give itself freely. And I am not willing to waste another minute pretending we have all the time in the world. We have right now. And right now, I choose you.

From my heart to yours

My Mom, Lakieta —

I have been carrying this in my heart for a while now, and I decided it was time to put it somewhere you could hold onto it. Somewhere you could come back to on a hard day and remember that you are deeply, completely, unconditionally loved.

You gave me life. But more than that — you gave me a foundation. You gave me the kind of love that a person carries with them into every room they walk into, every relationship they build, every hard moment they survive. I carry you with me everywhere I go, Mom. I always have.

Life has not been easy. For either of us. We have both been through storms that would have broken people who were not built the way we are built. But we are still here. Still standing. Still reaching for each other. And that means everything to me.

I need more time with you. Not because something is wrong — but because something is so right about the way I feel when I am with you. You are one of the few places in this world where I can just be Lil Renie. Not the fighter, not the one holding everything together — just your daughter. And I need that more than you know.

So this is me asking. Let's make the time. Let's choose each other on purpose. Let's not let another season go by without filling it with the kind of moments that matter — the real wins, the memories money can't buy, the simple beautiful ordinary magic of being together.

I love you more than I will ever be able to say. But I am going to keep trying.

Forever your Lil Renie 💜
